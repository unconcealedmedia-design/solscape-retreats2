Cavtat
Retreat

May 10–15, 2026  ·  6 Days

Overview

Five days on the Adriatic

Cavtat is one of the most beautiful towns on the Dalmatian coast — and one of the least visited. Its sheltered bay, crystal-clear water, and traffic-free promenade make it the perfect setting for a retreat that is equal parts movement and magic.

Each morning begins with yoga or barre on a sun-drenched terrace overlooking the sea. Afternoons open into island excursions, spa sessions, coastal swims, and long shared lunches. Evenings settle into sound baths, breathwork, and candlelit dinners as a small, close-knit group.

We keep groups deliberately small — never more than 10 people — because the intimacy is part of what makes SolScape work. By day two, you'll feel like you've known everyone for years.

Where You'll Stay

A villa above the bay

We stay in a beautifully restored villa perched above the old town of Cavtat, with a private terrace and pool looking directly out over the bay. Each room is simply furnished in white linen and natural wood — designed to rest the eye as much as the body.

The villa sleeps our group comfortably in private en-suite rooms. Breakfasts are taken communally on the terrace. Movement sessions happen on the shaded yoga deck as the morning light comes in from the sea. Evenings often drift from the sound bath to the dinner table to the water's edge.
