How It Started

Rooted in movement. Shaped by experience. Built with intention.

SolScape began through two different paths that slowly met in the same place.

Samantha was raised around yoga and holistic living from an early age, later deepening that foundation through years of personal practice and teaching. Nada came from a lifelong background in movement through ballet, fitness, and strength training, eventually expanding that journey into a broader exploration of wellness, balance, and what movement can become through different stages of life.

What brought them together was a shared appreciation for retreats that feel both grounding and intentional — experiences where movement, environment, and meaningful connection come together naturally.

Together, they began creating the kinds of retreats they themselves are drawn to: thoughtfully curated experiences rooted in movement, held within beautiful settings that encourage presence, rest, clarity, and reconnection.

Over the past three years, SolScape has moved through Egypt, Greece, Tuscany, and Spain — with each retreat shaping the next chapter of what the experience continues to become.

Our Philosophy

"Welcome to SolScape Retreats — where movement, adventure, and soulful living come together in the world's most breathtaking locations."

We believe in the power of yoga and barre to ground and energize, in adventure to awaken the spirit, and in soulful moments that create lasting transformation. Whether you're seeking deep relaxation, self-discovery, or a journey beyond the ordinary, we craft experiences that nourish the mind, body, and soul.

Join us as we explore, move, and connect — one retreat at a time.

The Founders

Meet Nada & Samantha

Samantha

Founder · One Yoga Academy

Samantha Howick is the founder of One Yoga Academy — artist, mother of four, and a multidisciplinary integrative mind–body educator. Her work is shaped by over three decades of immersive study and lived practice, including a background in gymnastics, yoga philosophy, contemplative inquiry, and embodied movement.

Alongside a Master's degree in Fine Art and Sculpture, this lineage has informed a teaching approach where yoga becomes a creative medium — each offering carefully curated as a lived experience rather than a prescribed form. She teaches from what she is living, not from a linear method. Her work invites people into embodied understanding — not only of how they move, but of what they feel, sense, and come to understand in their lives long after the practice ends.

@oneyogasamantha

Nada

Barre Instructor & Co-Founder

Nada Karame is a barre instructor and co-founder of SolScape Retreats, currently continuing her education through NASM in Women's Fitness and Behavior Change Specialization.

Her path has moved through fitness, business, and creative entrepreneurship, but movement has remained the constant thread throughout her life. From ballet to strength training to barre, her work is shaped by years of personal practice and a growing interest in how women move, adapt, and reconnect with themselves through different stages of life.

Through SolScape, Nada helps create thoughtfully curated retreats rooted in movement, travel, beauty, and meaningful connection.

@forbarre

What We Stand For

Three things we'll never compromise

Movement

Every retreat is built around real, structured physical practice. Not token yoga at sunrise and the rest of the day on a sunbed. We train. We breathe. We sweat. We rest properly because we've earned it.

Community

Small groups aren't a marketing decision — they're what makes SolScape work. You can't build anything real in a group of thirty strangers. Our retreats create conditions for connection, not the performance of it.

Sustainability

We work exclusively with properties that take environmental responsibility seriously. We keep groups small so footprints stay light. And we donate 2% of every booking to local reforestation and conservation.
